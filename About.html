History

Founding days

In the 10'th century, four powerful wizards and witches created Hogwarts Academy. These 4 founders are Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw and Salazar Slytherin.

Many years later...

At some point, pressure from Slytherin to only admit pure-blooded wizards started to greatly affect the other founders.

Links to Lord Voldemort

Later on, each of the founders would be linked with Lord Voldemort in some way due to him using things that belong to the founders for his Horcruxes.
